Process

What is it and How it works?

Facial Attractiveness is a system that will predict your appearance base on your face whether it see you attractive for it?

Data gathering

We randomly collect images of Asian model on the internet such Korean model, Chinese model, and Japanese model for 50 images. We also randomly select 50 images from SCUT-FBP dataset

Training

We use Caffe with is an open-source deep learning tool and CNN. We train our model with BVLC which is a provided model from Caffe.

Usage

Users will upload their picture with is a frontal face to the system. It will predict whether you are attractive to this AI or not ?
